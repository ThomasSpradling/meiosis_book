About

Not one person is the exact same. We're all unique! But why is that? Someone might answer "because we have different parents". Even though that is true, why are your parents different? Unlike humans, some animals don't have this ability to make children so different than themselves. For example, starfish clone themselves by popping off an arm that'll grow back into the entire animal. Why are we different?
